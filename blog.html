About Jihane Talibi

the face behind MiniCakes.

“ Welcome to MiniCake, where sweetness meets simplicity! I'm Jihane Talibi, the heart and hands behind every tiny delight. Driven by a passion for baking, I started MiniCake to share joy in every bite. From birthdays to everyday celebrations, our mini cakes are crafted with love and creativity. Each bite-sized treat is a moment of indulgence, making every occasion a bit more special. Join me on this delightful journey, and let's sweeten life one mini cake at a time. Sweetly, Jihane Talibi”

Why mini cakes? Well, I believe that good things come in small packages. These petite treats are not just desserts; they are a symbol of life's little celebrations, whether big or small. Birthdays, anniversaries, or simply a sweet craving – our mini cakes are here to add a touch of magic to your moments. What sets us apart is the dedication to craftsmanship and the personal touch infused into every creation. minicake.com is not just an online bakery; it's a celebration of the sweet moments that make life extraordinary.”
